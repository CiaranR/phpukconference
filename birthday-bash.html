10th Birthday Bash

Let's Celebrate!

Thursday February 19 — 6pm til midnight

Bowling • Dinner • Karaoke • Board Games • Cake • Prizes

To celebrate 10 years of the PHP UK Conference we've planned a fantastic party for all of our delegates.

Sponsored by

New Relic and Dream Factory

Location

We'll be heading to All Star Lanes bowling alley on historic Brick Lane in East London where we have exclusive hire of the entire venue.

Getting There

There will be busses running all delegates from the Brewery to All Star Lanes directly after the closing sessions, between 6-7pm. As you can imagine, it will take some time to get 700 people on a bus, so just head to the King George and grab a free drink while checking out the exhibitors until your bus is ready — thank you for your patience with this.

If you need to come to the venue later and will be making your own way, please remember to bring your badge in order to gain entry to the event.

The closest station to All Star Lanes is Shoreditch High Street.

Getting In

Your conference badge will be required for entrance. If you are attending on Friday only and do not have a badge yet, please give your name at the door and the doorman can check you in.

Unfortunately children under 18 years of age are not able to attend the event, due to licensing rules at the venue.

Activities

  • 9 bowling lanes
  • 2 private karaoke rooms
  • 3 pool tables
  • Wii
  • Board games
  • Dinner & dessert
  • Bars, tables, and couches to relax and hang out

Food & Drink

Since we are going straight from the conference to the social, we'll have a delicious dinner buffet.

And you can't have a birthday party without birthday cake!

There will be vegetarian options available. If you have other dietary requirements, please contact us with your order number and requirements.

Amenities

There is a free cloak room available and the entire venue is handicap accessible.

Cost

Free! Thanks to our awesome sponsors New Relic and Dream Factory, all activities, games and food are free, including bowling shoes, ball rental and karaoke. Drinks are not sponsored however, the bars accept both cash and card.

Staff

Since we have exclusive hire of the venue a full venue staff will be available to help you sign up for a bowling game, book a karaoke session, or answer any other questions. PHP UK staff will also be available on site to assist you.
