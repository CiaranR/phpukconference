Socials

An important part of any conference is being able to network with other delegates and PHP UK is no exception. We've arranged three social events - before, during and after the conference. Venues and exact times will be announced in early 2015.

Wednesday

Wednesday night kicks off the conference with a night at a pub just around the corner from the conference venue. Previously known as the Master Gunner, the newly rebranded Finch's is located opposite Finsbury square, close to Moorgate tube and Old Street Station.

Drinks are sponsored by PHP London. You can find more details about Finch's and their address on their website.

Thursday

On Thursday night we'll be hosting our birthday party at a really cool local venue. We'll have the place to ourselves all night so you can come and play some games, grab plenty of free food and make use of one of the multiple bars.

Full details will be available in the next few days.

Friday

On Friday night we will be hosting a post-conference social for all the conference delegates to hang out and relax as the event comes to a close. Check back nearer to the date for full information.
