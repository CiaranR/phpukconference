CONFERENCE SOCIALS

THURSDAY

Thursday night kicks off the conference with a night at the pub. Meet us at the Master Gunner on City Road at Finsbury Square from 7pm. The downstairs is booked exclusively for PHP UK attendees. The Master Gunner has a lovely selection of food and drinks and a comfortable atmosphere.

FRIDAY

Friday night we'll be hosting our mid-conference social at the conference venue. We'll start with a huge prize raffle, open for all delegates. We'll have free drinks, mini burgers, mini pizzas and snacks until 9pm giving you a chance to meet and network with all the other delegates and check out the exhibits too. If you're participating in the Hackathon or want to pop by and check it out, it'll be running until 11pm.

SATURDAY

Saturday night we'll be hosting our post-conference social at our PHP London monthly local The Fox, on Paul Street behind Old Street station. The whole pub is available for exclusive use from 6pm til close for all the conference delegates to hang out and relax as the event comes to a close.
