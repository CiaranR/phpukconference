Socials

An important part of any conference is being able to network with other delegates and PHP UK is no exception. We've arranged three social events - before, during and after the conference. Venues and exact times will be announced in early 2015.

Wednesday

Wednesday night kicks off the conference with a night at the pub. We'll be meeting at Finch's Pub on Finsbury Square (City Road) from 6pm til close for for drinks, snacks, and a chance to meet other delegates before the conference starts. Drinks are sponsored by PHP London.

Thursday

Thursday night we'll be hosting our mid-conference social at the conference venue. We'll start with a huge prize raffle, open for all delegates. We'll have free drinks, mini burgers, pizzas and snacks until 9pm giving you a chance to meet and network with all the other delegates and check out the exhibits too.

Friday

Friday night we'll be hosting our post-conference social for all the conference delegates to hang out and relax as the event comes to a close.
