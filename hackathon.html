Hackathon

Presented by Jetbrains

For the third time we will be hosting a Hackathon alongside the main tracks and, as it's our 10th birthday, this year it will be party themed!

We are looking for teams to produce web apps inspired by traditional birthday party games. Think pass the parcel, pin the tail on the donkey, musical chairs, etc.

There will be prizes awarded throughout the Hackathon for the most innovative hacks and the winners will get two free tickets to next year’s conference, PHP Storm licenses and more. There will also be the opportunity to present your hack on the main stage after the final talk to all the delegates.

Sign up your team now on Hackerleague!

The hackathon is sponsored by JetBrains who have a passion for making developers more productive. They design intelligent development tools to simplify your challenging tasks, automate the easy ones, and help you develop with pleasure.
